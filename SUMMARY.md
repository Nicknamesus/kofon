# Kofon Motion Group — Site Summary

## What They Do
Chinese B2B manufacturer of precision motion and transmission components for industrial automation. Headquartered in Hubei, China, with offices in Germany (Augsburg, Igersheim) and Korea (Seoul).

| Brand | Product |
