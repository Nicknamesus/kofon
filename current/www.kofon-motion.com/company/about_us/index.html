About Us


The KOFON® Motion Group possesses expertise for the mastery of high precision motion technologies. The group company was established in the year of 2010. Now it employs over 1080 staffs worldwide.

KOFON® Motion Group specializes in the development, manufacturing and marketing of the following high precision motion brands product lines:

KOFON® | CaesarPlanetary®: Precision Planetary Gearbox and Pinion

KOFON® | CaesarSpiral®: Precision Spiral Bevel Gearbox

KOFON® | Elitewave®: Ultra Precision Strain Wave Gear

KOFON® | Rollsate®: Precision Planetary Roller Screw

KOFON® | KGV® Solutions: Gearbox Wheel Solutions for AMRs

KOFON® | Servolux®: Precision Servo Motor Solutions

Our reliable core competence automatic industries and including precision tooling machines, AGV/AMR precision motion systems, semi-conductor industry, medical automation industry, robotics precision motion systems and new energy industry, etc.

KOFON® Motion aims to be dedicated to serve global automation industrial clients with the reliable precision motion solutions.
